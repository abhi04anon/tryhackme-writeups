Why Methodology Matters in Penetration Testing
Press enter or click to view image in full size

In professional penetration testing, success rarely comes from a single exploit.

It comes from:

    Understanding scope and authorization
    Gathering intelligence systematically
    Interpreting technical findings
    Connecting weaknesses into attack paths
    Communicating risk clearly

Without methodology, testing becomes random experimentation. With methodology, it becomes disciplined security assessment.

This room was built to teach that discipline.
Room Overview

    Analytical thinking
    Professional workflows
    Decision-making
    Risk evaluation
    Reporting mindset

The goal is to prepare learners for real security work, not just CTF competitions.
